Job Description

Data analyst is responsible for collecting, organizing and maintaining our master data set along with building, developing and providing periodically, or upon request, the relevant business insights, reports, dashboards that assist in proper decision making to achieve rapid business growth and strong performance management of FFM KPIs. Experience as a data analyst, working with big data, deep understanding of data analysis tools and managing databases is essential to succeed in this role.

Key Responsibilities
  • Interpret master complex data sets, analyze results using statistical techniques via data analysis and reporting tools
  • Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
  • Provide quality assurance of imported data to ensure data integrity
  • Managing and designing the reporting environment, including data sources, security, and metadata.
  • Locate and define new process improvement opportunities
  • Generating reports from single or multiple systems.
  • Troubleshooting the reporting database environment and reports.
